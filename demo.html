Spot App Web Demo

Test the idea
on your projector.

This demo lets you test brightness, placement, control feel, and basic adjustments in your actual room. It starts with a short branded loading screen, then unlocks the normal spot for a limited session.

• Click or tap: turn the spot on and off.
• Mouse, trackpad, or 1 finger: move the spot.
• Scroll wheel or 2 fingers up/down: resize the spot.
• Right click + scroll or 3 fingers up/down: soften or sharpen the edge.
• Number keys: change white tone. R, G, B, Y, C, M, W: quick colors.
Press Escape anytime to leave fullscreen.
