Projection lighting, simplified

Turn any projector
into a follow spot.

Spot App is built around one simple production problem. Sometimes you just need to put the focus where it belongs. In a perfect world, every school, church, and small venue would have a real follow spot and a trained operator ready to go. In the real world, most of us have an old projector, a tight budget, and somebody asking if we can make it work by Friday. Spot takes the projector you already have and turns it into a simple way to guide attention on stage.

The choice

Buy another machine.
Or use the one you already have.

A real follow spot can be great, but it is also expensive, bulky, and not always worth it for a venue that only needs it a few times a year. That is the gap Spot is trying to fill. Instead of buying another piece of gear that may sit in storage most of the year, Spot lets you use a projector you may already own and control the spotlight effect through software. It is not trying to replace a full professional lighting system. It is giving schools, churches, and smaller venues a practical option when the other option is having no follow spot at all.

See it work

Move your finger.
Move the light.

The goal is for Spot to feel simple from the first time you touch it. Connect the device to a projector, aim it at the stage, and move the light with your finger. Drag it where you need it, adjust the size, soften the edge, change the color, and keep the focus where the moment needs it. No giant learning curve, no complicated booth setup, and no pretending every volunteer wants to become a lighting engineer overnight. If only the rest of the show was this easy.
